Club Week

Club week is usually the second week of school. Throughout this club, different clubs will advertise there club's purpose and try to attract members. This week is a great way for the student body to join new clubs and for the freshmen to see the huge variety of clubs we have in our school.

Tailgate

Tailgate happens right before the homecoming game. ASB has multiple stations, for example last year we even had a zipline! The tailgate is a wonderful way for your club to raise money. Your club can hold game stations, face paint stations, etc. In previous years, clubs help cougar pride stations, hyping the student body for the game, pie in the face games, and even a basketball hoop game. Clubs, however, cannot sell food.

Winterfest

Winterfest is the biggest event for clubs! Perfomance clubs such as our Polynesian club, perform a dance piece, as well as other clubs raising money for their club. Clubs can sell food, have game stations, or even hold a photo booth! Each club will be assigned a table by ICC. About two weeks prior to the event, a Google Doc will be released and your club can sign up!

Club officers can hold any other fundraiser,performance, activity, etc by turning in an ERF.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can we sell food?

Your club can only sell food 30 minutes after school.

How can we make sure that we are re-chartered next year?

Make sure your club has regular meetings, at least once a month, and at least 8 members present in each meeting.

What is our club advisor is absent or on a long leave?

As long as your club has finds a teacher who can supervise you for your meetings you can continue holding meetings.

What is the role of the club liaison?

The club liaison is there to make sure you are holding meetings, collect a PRINT OUT of your club minutes, and will evaluate what you have done all year.

Who can we contact if we have any other questions?
